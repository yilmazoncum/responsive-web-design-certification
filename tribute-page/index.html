Hasan Âli Yücel

Hasan Âli Yücel (17 December 1897 - 26 February 1961) was a Turkish education reformer and philosophy teacher who served as minister of national education of Turkey from December 1938 to August 1946. He is remembered for the foundation of Village Institutes.

Accomplishments

  • Turkey's entry into UNESCO and foundation of State Conservatory (20 May 1940) has been a result of his efforts
  • Translation of world classics
  • Preliminary studies of İnönü Encyclopedia, the first official and copyrighted Turkish encyclopedia
  • The University Reform (establishment of Ankara University Faculty of Science, transformation of Higher Engineering School into Istanbul Technical University and establishment of Ankara University Faculty of Medicine),
  • Establishment of Village Institutes
